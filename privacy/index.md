# Stringly — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-04-28

Stringly ("we", "the app") is a badminton string-tracking application built by Stringly Studio.

## What we collect

**On your device only:**
- Rackets, strings, tensions, dates, notes, and break logs you enter. This data is stored locally in the app's database (SQLite) and never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

**Anonymous usage analytics (sent to PostHog):**
- A randomly-generated device ID (not linked to your name, email, or phone number)
- App version, OS, device model
- Anonymous events: app opens, screens viewed, racket/log creation counts, feature usage, errors
- Approximate country (derived from IP at request time; we do not store IPs)

We do **not** collect: your name, email, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any health/fitness data.

## What we do not do
- We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
- We do not show ads.
- We do not track you across other apps or websites.
- We do not use your data to train machine-learning models.

## Third-party services and data residency

| Processor | Purpose | Data shared | Default region |
|---|---|---|---|
| **PostHog** | Anonymous product analytics | Anonymous device ID, app/OS version, anonymous events | US (`us.i.posthog.com`) |
| **Sentry** | Anonymous crash reports | Stack traces, OS/device info, no PII | US |
| **Apple App Store / TestFlight** | App distribution | Per Apple's policy | Apple regional |
| **Google Play** | App distribution | Per Google's policy | Google regional |

**EU/UK users:** PostHog and Sentry process data in the United States under Standard Contractual Clauses. If you require EU-region processing, contact kevinliu217@gmail.com before installing.

## Your rights (GDPR / UK GDPR / CCPA)

- **Access (Art. 15):** Settings → Privacy & Data → "Export my data" produces a JSON of all on-device data instantly. For analytics, email kevinliu217@gmail.com with your anonymous ID (Settings → "My analytics ID"); response within 30 days.
- **Erasure (Art. 17):** on-device data via Settings → "Delete all local data" (instant); analytics deletion via email request, completed within 30 days.
- **Portability (Art. 20):** "Export my data" emits a structured JSON snapshot.
- **Rectification (Art. 16):** edit any record directly in the app.
- **Opt out (Art. 21 / CCPA):** Settings → toggle off "Send anonymous analytics." Effective immediately.
- **Complaints:** EU residents may contact their DPA (https://edpb.europa.eu); UK residents https://ico.org.uk.

## Children
Stringly is not directed at children under 13 (under 16 in the EU/UK). We do not knowingly collect data from children.
